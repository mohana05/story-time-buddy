# PRIVACY POLICY FOR STORYTIME BUDDY

**Last Updated: December 2025**

StoryTime Buddy ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting the
privacy of children and families who use our app. This Privacy Policy
explains how we handle information when you use **StoryTime Buddy: AI
Stories** (the "App").

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 1. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

We designed StoryTime Buddy with privacy as a core principle. Here's
what you need to know:

### 1.1 Information We *DO NOT* Collect

-   We do **NOT** collect personal information from children
-   We do **NOT** collect names, email addresses, or contact information
-   We do **NOT** collect photos or images of users
-   We do **NOT** track location data
-   We do **NOT** use cookies or tracking technologies
-   We do **NOT** share any data with third parties
-   We do **NOT** serve advertisements

### 1.2 Information Stored Locally on Your Device

The following data is stored **only on your device** and never
transmitted to us:

-   Stories you create (text and images)
-   Character preferences and settings
-   Reading progress and favorites
-   App preferences (language, voice settings, font choices)
-   Purchase history (managed by Apple)

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 2. AI PROCESSING

### 2.1 On-Device AI

StoryTime Buddy uses **Apple Intelligence** for story and image
generation. All AI processing occurs:

-   Entirely on your device
-   Without sending data to external servers
-   Using Apple's built-in machine learning frameworks

### 2.2 No Cloud Processing

Your stories, prompts, and creative content are:

-   Never uploaded to cloud servers
-   Never used to train AI models
-   Never shared with Apple or third parties

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 3. CHILDREN'S PRIVACY (COPPA COMPLIANCE)

StoryTime Buddy is designed for children ages 4--10 and complies with
the **Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA)**.

### 3.1 We Do Not:

-   Collect personal information from children under 13
-   Require account creation or registration
-   Enable social features, chat, or communication with others
-   Display behavioral advertising
-   Share children's data with third parties

### 3.2 Parental Controls

-   Parents can delete all stored data at any time
-   Parents can restrict in-app purchases via iOS settings
-   No parental consent is required since we collect no data

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 4. IN-APP PURCHASES

### 4.1 Purchase Handling

-   All purchases are processed by Apple through the App Store
-   We do not have access to payment information
-   Purchase history is managed by Apple, not us

### 4.2 Family Sharing

-   Our in-app purchases support Apple Family Sharing
-   Parents can share purchases with family members
-   Parents can control purchase permissions via Screen Time

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 5. DATA STORAGE AND SECURITY

### 5.1 Local Storage Only

All app data is stored locally on your device using:

-   iOS secure storage mechanisms
-   Apple's SwiftData framework
-   Standard iOS file protection

### 5.2 Data Deletion

You can delete all app data by:

-   Deleting individual stories within the app
-   Using the **"Clear All Data"** option in Settings
-   Uninstalling the app from your device

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 6. DEVICE PERMISSIONS

StoryTime Buddy may request the following permissions:

### 6.1 Microphone (Optional)

-   Used for voice input when creating stories
-   Audio is processed on-device only
-   Never recorded, stored, or transmitted

### 6.2 Camera (Optional)

-   Used to scan text from books or documents
-   Images are processed on-device only
-   Never stored or transmitted

### 6.3 Photo Library (Optional)

-   Used to extract text from saved images
-   Images are processed on-device only
-   Never uploaded or shared

### 6.4 Speech Recognition (Optional)

-   Uses Apple's on-device speech recognition
-   Voice data stays on your device
-   Not sent to Apple or third parties

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 7. THIRD-PARTY SERVICES

### 7.1 Apple Services

We use the following Apple services:

-   App Store (for purchases)
-   Apple Intelligence (for AI features)
-   Text-to-Speech (for narration)

These services follow Apple's Privacy Policy:\
https://www.apple.com/privacy/

### 7.2 No Other Third Parties

We do NOT use:

-   Analytics services
-   Advertising networks
-   Social media integrations
-   Crash reporting services
-   Any external APIs or servers

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 8. INTERNATIONAL USERS

StoryTime Buddy is available worldwide. Regardless of your location:

-   All data stays on your device
-   No data is transferred across borders
-   We comply with GDPR, COPPA, and other privacy regulations
